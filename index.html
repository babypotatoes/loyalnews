 THE
 LOYAL
 COLLECTIVE

Founded on uniting young creatives and providing them with solid foundations, The Loyal Collective identifies as a music record label that builds a platform for creatives to launch their passions on the world's biggest platforms.

The Loyal Collective was founded December 17th of 2016 under the working title 'UNITED' and officially launched September 4th of 2017 as The Loyal Collective.

                                                PO/LAR

PO/LAR was originally built to provide a simple and effective way to submit items for publishing under The Loyal Collective. It evolved into a platform that allows anyone and everyone to take advantage of the world's largest stages without being signed to The Loyal Collective.

PO/LAR began development April 17th of 2019. A private alpha was launched the following month on May 18th. PO/LAR entered a public beta on August 21st of 2019 and is still under development.

 SYMMETRY

Turn creative daydreams into realities with your own team behind you. Symmetry partners with organizations and individuals to fund and support their projects. 

Symmetry was created as program under The Loyal Collective on September 28th of 2018 and then founded as a separate entity on January 15th of 2019. 

                                                     RA|N

Utilizing modern and sleek designs, RA|N is a clothing brand built on giving back to the world through ecological charities and foundations. Our planet is our home and our only home. Instead of looking to the stars for other homes, we need to restore our planet before it is too late.

RA|N was founded on July 22nd of 2019 and has yet to launch its first product line.

 MINT

Mint is currently being kept a secret until launch. We at Loyal are extremely excited to show off this new adventure we're embarking on. Mint is very different than anything Loyal has done so far and we know you will love it.

Mint was founded January 28th of 2019 and has yet to launch.
